Meet Your Instructor

Guided by Meenu Somani

Meenu Somani founded Energy Centre to make authentic, therapeutic yoga accessible from anywhere in the world. With over a decade of teaching experience across beginner, therapeutic, and prenatal yoga, she blends traditional Hatha technique with a gentle, modern approach that meets every student exactly where they are.

Her classes are known for unhurried pacing, clear instruction, and genuine attention to each student's body and breath — whether you're stepping onto the mat for the first time or working through years of built-up tension.
